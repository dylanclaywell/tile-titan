Tile Layers

Tile Layer Functionality

Tile Titan's Tile Layer functionality allows users to create visually rich maps by painting tiles onto layers. With support for uploading tilemaps from images and adding and erasing tiles, this feature provides a versatile and efficient way to design simple yet intricate landscapes.

Uploading Tilemaps

To get started with the Tile Layer, follow these steps to upload tilemaps from images:

  1. Navigate to the Tile Layer:

    • Open your project in Tile Titan and select the desired tile layer from the layer options.

  1. Upload Tilemap Image:
    • Click on the "New tileset" button.
    • Choose an image file containing your desired tileset.
    • Tile Titan will automatically extract individual tiles from the image and display them in the tileset palette.

Adding Tiles

Once the tilemap is uploaded, use the following steps to add tiles to the selected layer:

  1. Select a Tile:

    • Choose a tile from the tileset palette. Tile Titan will indicate the currently selected tile over the tile canvas in the main view.

  2. Adding Tiles:

    • Hover over the canvas, and the cursor will change to the selected tile.
    • Click and drag to paint tiles onto the layer.
    • Adding tiles will automatically be snapped to the tile grid.

Erasing Tiles

To remove tiles from the layer, follow these steps:

  1. Select Eraser Tool:

    • Switch to the eraser tool in the toolbar.

  2. Erase Tiles:

    • Click on the tiles you want to erase on the layer.

Tips and Tricks

  • Layer Visibility:

    • You can click the Show/Hide button on the layer to toggle visibility

  • Save Regularly:

    • Save your project regularly to avoid losing progress.
